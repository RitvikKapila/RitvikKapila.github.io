About Me

In March 2024, I graduated with a Master's degree in Computer Science at the University of California San Diego with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence, especially privacy-preserving ML, and time-series modeling, with a cumulative GPA of 4.0/4.0.

I completed my undergraduate degree (B.Tech) at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IITD), in the Electrical Engineering Department in 2021.
